## What is HiveLog?

HiveLog is a simple app for logging hive events in the field. It's built for beekeepers wearing gloves — big buttons, high contrast, minimal typing. It works offline and syncs when you get signal back.

---

## What you can do right now (v1)

### Yards
- **View all your yards** on the Home screen
- **Add a yard** with the orange + button (name + optional location note)
- **Search yards** by name or location
- **Tap a yard** to see its colonies

### Colonies (Hives)
- **View all colonies** in a yard with color-coded status dots:
  - Green = inspected in the last 30 days
  - Yellow = 31–60 days since last inspection
  - Red = 60+ days or marked dead out
  - Grey = never inspected
- **Add colonies one at a time** or **batch add** (e.g., create 48 hives labeled H-001 through H-048)
- **Mark a colony as Dead Out** by tapping the status badge on its detail page
- **Search colonies** by label

### Logging Events
- **Tap a colony** to see its event history, then tap **"Log Event"**
- Pick from 7 event types (one tap each):
  - Inspection, Treatment, Feed, Split, Loss, Requeen, Harvest
- Add optional notes (the only place you need to type)
- **Batch log for an entire yard** — tap "Log Event for Yard" on the colony list to apply one event to all active colonies at once (great for yard-wide treatments)

### Editing
- Go to **Settings** to rename yards, update location notes, or rename colony labels — all inline editing
- **Sign out** is at the bottom of Settings

### Offline
- The app works fully offline — create yards, add colonies, log events with no signal
- A banner appears at the top when you're offline
- Everything syncs automatically when you reconnect

## How to install on your phone

1. Open https://hivelog-tawny.vercel.app in Safari (iPhone) or Chrome (Android)
2. **iPhone:** Tap the Share button > "Add to Home Screen"
3. **Android:** Tap the 3-dot menu > "Install app" or "Add to Home Screen"
4. Now it opens like a regular app and works offline

## Quick walkthrough

1. **Log in** at the URL above
2. You'll see the **My Yards** screen (may be empty if this is a fresh account)
3. Tap the **+** button to create your first yard
4. Inside the yard, tap **+** to add colonies (try batch mode for speed)
5. Tap a colony > **Log Event** > pick an event type > Save
6. That's it — you're logging
